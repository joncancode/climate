Secure Community Rights and Management

Indigenous and local communities’ land rights and management of their forests, as well as a stop to the killing and criminalization of environmental defenders.

Deforestation and Conflict-Free Commodities

Elimination of deforestation, land degradation, illegality, and social conflict from global supply chains and related financial investments.

High Standards for Infrastructure and Extractive Industries

High environmental, social, and governance standards, and increased transparency and oversight of investments in infrastructure and extractive industries.

Strategic Climate and Land Use Communications

Increased understanding of sustainable forests and lands as essential to people and the planet to improve policy outcomes and land use practices.

Natural Carbon Capture

The role of forest carbon sinks are increased, while ensuring forests retain ecosystem, cultural, and other values for local communities.

Healthy Diets

Consumption is shifted away from meat sources with high carbon footprint toward options that are healthier for people and the planet.
